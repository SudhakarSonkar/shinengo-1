About Flood Campaign

Gorakhpur is a zone four area and is tarai due to River Rapti, hence it is prone to face floods and earthquakes. Our team has an extensive experience in disaster risk management.

Gorakhpur flood 2017:
We have worked in evacuation of flooded villages and donated clean water and one day nutritious meal for five days. The donation was first started off by our own volunteers' contribution later many effluent people poured in their resources and service in cooking meals everyday. Our experience in that disaster mitigation of 2014 has helped us in covid-19 times where we joined hands with city Dm office and community kitchens to distribute medicine to locked patients, food to footloose migrants and footpath sleeperes.
